A visit to Dandi Beach, Near Navsari

May,21 2017

Why Dandi Beach is popular ?

In 1930,Mahatma Gandhi undertook the Salt Satyagraha protesting against the British salt laws.He Walked from Ahmedabad with a group of followers that kept growing at each stage of his protest march. At Dandi, he plucked a handful of saline mud and seawater, producing salt in producing salt in protest against the British monopoly on salt production.This simple act got much national support and is credited with breaking the back of the Raj in India.

There is a small museum that shows some events of Mahatma Gandhiji's Life. There is a statue of Mahatma Gandhi having salt in his hand. This statue marks the location where he first broke the salt law in 1930.

Dandi Beach is a nice place for picnic with friends or family or with loved once. When we visited this place there was summer going on and we expirienced a really heated sand and alomost so hot weather. So I think its good to go in the evening and early morning to this place.

As there is no public transportation in this area a long way, to reach there you must have your own vehicle. There are some food stalls so you dont have to worry about that.

No more crowed area here so if someone need piece of mind can visit this place and can enjoy the sunset in the evening.
